About Us

Home to a state-of-the-art, floodlit cricket turf featuring advanced practice nets and cutting-edge tracking technology for player improvement. Complemented by a relaxing refreshment corner, a pro shop for cricket gear, and spacious seating for spectators. There's truly something for everyone, with facilities for friendly matches, coaching sessions, and thrilling tournaments for players of all skill levels.
